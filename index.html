    An Overview of Game Design and Who I Am:

    Hi welcome to my website! This site should serve as a jumping off point for your game development research. Whether you are a hobbiest or an aspiring professional everyone has to start somewhere. I am Ethan Nankervis and I began getting into game design in the summer of 2020, I begun much the same as you and was quickly overwhelmed by just how much you can do within game design, it put me off of developing for a while. Eventually I had managed to scrounge up enough knowledge through obscure forums and youtubers to be able to make my own game. I have made this website in the hopes that you will not need to go through the hassel that I endured to start learning how to make games. This site should serve as an all inclusive way to get a background on game development.

    Interested in getting started?

    Game design may seem like a ginormous task, so I have worked to collect some information to help you get on your way with info about game engines, genres, and some advanced tips for you to think about before you start development

    Learn about Game Jams

    Game Jams are events hosted by people and companies where you have a limited amount of time to create a game following a given theme.
